Music Creation in VR

Music in VR was the given title of my Dissertation

Music Creation in VR has many channels that you can dive into, I chose to create a virtual music suite, based on a music studio where you could walk around and pick up any instrument that you want.


The first instrument that I created was a large Drum machine designed to look like shop shelves to keep familiarty with users.

The Final version of the software included:
    Drum Machine
    Drum Kit
    Guitar
    Keyboard
    Tooltips + Hints
    On-Hand Drum Menu
    Bin for spare Drum pieces

The software was Created using Unity and C# Along with an HTC VIVE, Oculus Quest, SteamVR and ALVR for wireless connection over Wifi to my system.
